Al-Fitrah is a dedicated organization committed to fostering community development, supporting underprivileged individuals, and promoting sustainable growth through education, welfare, and self-sufficiency initiatives. Rooted in Islamic values, we strive to restore authentic Islamic knowledge, making it accessible and impactful for learners and educators.

Al-Fitrah is open Most days except on Thursday and closes at 4:30 PM. For any updates on our working hours, feel free to contact us.
