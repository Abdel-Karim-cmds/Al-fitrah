A Vision of Faith and Empowerment

Founded by Ustadha Amina Abdiqadir an educator, entrepreneur, with over 30 year sof experience focusing on empowering Muslim youth and women.

Mission

To restore the essence of Islamic knowledge, making it authentic, accessible, and impactful, while cultivating a generation of educators and learners who uphold truth and inspire transformation.

Vission
