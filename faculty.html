Ustadah Amina
Faculty & Professional Profile

Ustadah Amina is a distinguished educator with over 20 years of experience in education and personal development. As the founder of Al-Fitrah Learning Centre and a certified Mental Health First Aid provider, she is an expert in cultural education, diversity training, and motivational speaking.

  • Certified Mental Health First Aid provider
  • Expert in cultural education & diversity training
  • Professional speaker and life coach

Core Competencies

  • Classroom management and teacher training
  • Public speaking and motivational guidance
  • Individual and group counseling
  • Youth mentorship and parent-teacher engagement
